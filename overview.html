About Us

Welcome to Natanam Foundation, a premier dance school dedicated to teaching the traditional Indian dance form of Bharathnatyam. We are committed to preserving and promoting this ancient art form through high-quality instruction, performances, and exams.

Our Classes

At Natanam Foundation, we offer a variety of classes for all skill levels. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced dancer aiming to refine your skills, our experienced instructors are here to guide you every step of the way.

Exams

We believe in recognizing the hard work and dedication of our students. Our dance school conducts regular exams to assess the progress of our students and provide them with certification of their achievements. These exams are a great way to measure growth and celebrate milestones.

Performances

Natanam Foundation also has a troupe of skilled performers available for events. Our performers have graced numerous stages and are known for their captivating performances that bring the rich tradition of Bharathnatyam to life. Contact us to book our dancers for your next event.
